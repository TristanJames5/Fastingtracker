🏋️ Fasting Tracker
Start/stop button for fasting windows: This is the core interaction. The user taps “Start Fast” when they begin their fasting period, and the app records the exact timestamp. Later, when they tap “Stop Fast,” the app calculates the total duration. This simple toggle ensures that fasting sessions are tracked accurately without requiring manual input.

Timer that shows how long you’ve been fasting: While fasting is active, a live timer runs in the background, displaying hours and minutes. This gives real‑time feedback, motivating the user to push through hunger and stay disciplined. It’s like watching the clock during a workout set — you see progress happening in front of you.

Graphs to visualize weekly progress: Beyond individual sessions, the app generates charts showing fasting durations across days and weeks. These visualizations help users spot patterns, measure consistency, and celebrate improvements. For example, a bar graph could highlight which days had longer fasts, reinforcing accountability.

💧 Water Intake Log
Quick‑add buttons (250ml, 500ml, 1L): To make logging effortless, the app provides preset buttons for common amounts. Instead of typing numbers, users just tap the button that matches their bottle size. This reduces friction and encourages consistent tracking.

Daily goal meter that fills up like a protein shaker: A progress bar represents hydration goals. As water is logged, the bar fills, visually resembling a shaker bottle being topped up. This gamified design makes hydration feel rewarding and tangible.

Notifications: “Hydrate, champ!”: Timely reminders nudge users to drink water throughout the day. Instead of generic alerts, the app uses motivational gym‑bro language to keep the vibe fun and encouraging. These notifications prevent dehydration and reinforce discipline.

📅 Daily Streak Checklist
Customizable habits (fasting, water, workout, sleep): Users can define which habits they want to track daily. The checklist isn’t limited to fasting and hydration — it can include workouts, sleep quality, or journaling. This flexibility makes the app a holistic lifestyle tracker.

Streak counter with fire 🔥 animation when you don’t break it: Each day a habit is completed, the streak counter increases. To make it visually exciting, the counter is paired with fire animations, symbolizing momentum and energy. The longer the streak, the more intense the animation, reinforcing consistency.

Reset if you miss a day — accountability like a gym buddy: If a habit isn’t completed, the streak resets to zero. This strict reset system mirrors the accountability of a workout partner — miss a session, and you start over. It instills discipline and prevents complacency.

💪 Gym Bro Vibes
Motivational quotes when you log progress: Each time a user completes a task, the app displays a short motivational phrase. Instead of sterile confirmations, it delivers hype messages like “Stay anabolic, king” or “Hydration = gains.” These quotes inject personality and keep users engaged.

UI theme: bold fonts, dark mode with neon highlights (like a gym at night): The design aesthetic reflects gym culture — strong, bold typography paired with dark backgrounds and neon accents. It feels like stepping into a modern fitness studio, aligning the app’s look with its purpose.

Progress badges: “Shredded Discipline,” “Hydration Beast,” “Streak Machine.”: Achievements gamify the experience. As users hit milestones, they unlock badges with gym‑bro titles. These badges act as digital trophies, celebrating discipline and consistency.
